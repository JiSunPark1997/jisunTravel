OKASA & KYOTO

View Images

Osaka(大阪市, Ōsaka-shi) is second largest metropolitan area after Tokyo. It does not have many tourism resources comparing to Kyoto and Nara, but it has many historical sites, such as Osaka Catle and Shotoku (oldest temple in Japan).

Kyoto(京都, Kyōto) served as Japan's capital and the emperor's residence from 794 until 1868. It has many beautiful classical Buddhist temples, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses.

Overall, Japan was really clean, quiet, and charming place. People were really nice and polite. My favorite part was their food. All the Japanese foods were so delicious and unforgettable. Japan is the place I would definately visit once more.
